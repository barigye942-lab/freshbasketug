About Us 🌿

Bringing Uganda's Freshest Produce to Your Doorstep

We are Fresh Basket UG — a student-led digital marketplace connecting Kampala residents with local farmers for the freshest fruits and vegetables, delivered fast.

Our Story

How Fresh Basket UG Started

Fresh Basket UG was founded by a passionate team of Business Administration students at Makerere University's College of Business and Management Sciences.

We noticed a common problem among our fellow students, working professionals, and families in Kampala — getting fresh, affordable produce meant spending hours at crowded markets, often with no guarantee of quality.

We created Fresh Basket UG to bridge that gap. Our platform connects urban consumers directly to trusted local farmers, cutting out the middlemen and making fresh produce affordable, accessible, and convenient for everyone.

"Our mission is simple — no one in Kampala should have to choose between convenience and eating fresh. We make both possible."

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us Every Day

🎯

Our Mission

To provide fresh, high-quality fruits and vegetables through an easy-to-use online platform, offering convenience, reliability, and doorstep delivery to customers in Kampala and beyond.

🌍

Our Vision

To become Uganda's leading online fresh produce marketplace — empowering local farmers, improving food access for urban communities, and setting the standard for quality and convenience in digital grocery shopping.
