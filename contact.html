FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? ▼
You can place an order through our Order page, via WhatsApp on +256 700 000 000, or by calling us directly.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo and Airtel Money) and cash on delivery.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Orders placed before 12:00 PM are delivered same-day. Orders after 12 PM are delivered the following morning by 9:00 AM.
What if my produce is not fresh upon delivery? ▼
We guarantee freshness. If any item is unsatisfactory, contact us within 2 hours of delivery and we'll replace it or give a full refund.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders? ▼
Yes! We cater for restaurants, hostels, schools, and families buying in bulk. Contact us for special pricing on large orders.
